Understanding Your Heel Pain

Learn about the common causes and symptoms of heel pain and plantar fasciitis

Morning Pain

Sharp heel pain when taking your first steps in the morning is a classic sign of plantar fasciitis inflammation.

Activity-Related

Pain that worsens with prolonged standing, walking, or running activities, especially on hard surfaces.

Arch Tension

Tightness and discomfort along the arch of the foot, often extending from heel to toes.

Chronic Nature

Persistent pain that doesn't improve with rest and may gradually worsen without proper treatment.
